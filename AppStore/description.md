# App Store Listing

## Title
Liminal

## Subtitle
Find the rule. Find the exit.

## Description
Seven abstract spaces. No instructions. No tutorials. No HUD.

Each space operates by a hidden rule — a relationship between your movement, the light, the sound, and the geometry around you. Discover the rule through observation and experimentation. Find the exit by mastering it.

Liminal is a first-person exploration game that trusts you to figure things out. Every space responds differently to how you move, where you stand, how fast you go, and which direction you face. The world itself is your only guide.

• 7 unique spaces, each with distinct physics-based rules
• Real-time shader effects driven by your movement
• Spatial audio that responds to your position
• No text, no markers, no hand-holding
• Premium experience — no ads, no in-app purchases

Designed for players who enjoy atmospheric puzzles, spatial reasoning, and the satisfaction of understanding through experimentation.
